The Best Series

House of the Dragon

A spin-off of Game of Thrones, based on the book Fire & Blood by George R. R. Martin. Set 200 years before Game of Thrones, it tells the story of House Targaryen, a family that ruled the seven kingdoms and had great dragons. During the civil war, known as the Dance of the Dragons, they gradually enter into internal conflicts and lose their power.
Available on: HBO Max

Ted Lasso

Starring acclaimed comedian Jason Sudeikis, the Apple TV original series “Ted Lasso” follows the story of an American soccer coach who is hired to run a soccer team in England. Despite his inexperience with the particularities of soccer, a sport that is not so popular in the United States, Lasso gradually manages to win over the players and grow in the British league. Available on: Appel TV+
